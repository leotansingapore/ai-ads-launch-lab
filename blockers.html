Run all three prompts once on a FREE ChatGPT account

Your room will mostly be on free accounts. Signed-out ChatGPT can't create files (verified) and your paid account can (verified). Free tier is the unverified middle, and it's what students will actually be holding.

  1. Log into any free ChatGPT account (or make one at chatgpt.com, 2 minutes) — a colleague's works too
  2. New chat, paste Prompt 1, hit enter, wait for the guide:
I'm a financial advisor in Singapore. Help me write a short lead magnet PDF (5-7 key points) on the topic: 5 Retirement Mistakes Singaporeans Make Before 40.

My target audience is working adults aged 25-40 in Singapore.

Keep the tone practical, friendly, and jargon-free. Include a brief intro, the main points, and a closing that invites them to book a free consultation.
  1. Then Prompt 1b — the test that matters. You want it to return a .html file to download:

Design: navy blue and gold, modern, generous spacing, large readable text. A full-page cover with the title and "A free guide by Leo Tan", one section per point, and a closing page with a "Book your free consultation" call to action plus my contact: 91234567 / leo@example.com.

Make it print-perfect: A4 pages, each section starting on a new page, print styles included. Give me the finished .html file to download.
  1. Then Prompt 1c (the cover-image prompt from the card above) — you want an image to render
  2. Note anything that refused, asked for an upgrade, or hit a limit

Then tell Claude: "free tier: 1b gave a file / refused, 1c generated / refused" — if anything fails, the deck's recovery slide gets adjusted before workshop day, not during it.
