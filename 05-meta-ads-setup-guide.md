# How to Set Up Meta Ads Manager -- Step by Step

A visual guide for workshop attendees. Time needed: ~10 minutes.

---

## Step 1: Create a Meta Business Account

1. Go to **business.facebook.com**
2. Click **"Create Account"**
3. Enter your business name (e.g., "[Your Name] Financial Advisory")
4. Enter your name and email
5. Click **"Submit"**

[SCREENSHOT: business.facebook.com create account page]

---

## Step 2: Access Ads Manager

1. Go to **adsmanager.facebook.com**
2. If prompted, select the Business Account you just created
3. You should see an empty Ads Manager dashboard

[SCREENSHOT: Empty Ads Manager dashboard]

---

## Step 3: Add a Payment Method

1. In Ads Manager, click the **gear icon** (bottom left) or go to **Billing & Payments**
2. Click **"Payment Methods"**
3. Click **"Add Payment Method"**
4. Enter your credit or debit card details
5. Click **"Save"**

You need at least **$20 available** on the card. Your actual ad spend will be $5-10/day and you can pause or stop anytime.

[SCREENSHOT: Billing & Payments > Add Payment Method]

---

## Step 4: Verify Your Account is Ready

Go back to the main Ads Manager page (adsmanager.facebook.com). You should see:
- A green "Active" status or an empty campaign list
- No warning banners about restricted accounts

If you see a warning or restriction, see the troubleshooting section below.

[SCREENSHOT: Clean Ads Manager dashboard with no warnings]

---

## Troubleshooting

### "Your account is restricted"
- Go to **Settings > Identity Confirmation**
- Follow the identity verification steps (may require uploading an ID)
- This can take 24-48 hours -- do it NOW, not the day before the workshop

### "You don't have permission"
- Make sure you're logged into the correct Facebook account
- Go to business.facebook.com and check you're in the right Business Account

### "Payment method declined"
- Try a different card
- Make sure international transactions are enabled on your card
- Some Singapore bank cards need you to enable online payments via the bank's app

---

## You're Done!

Once you see a clean Ads Manager dashboard with a payment method linked, you're ready for the workshop. Tick this off your Pre-Flight Checklist!
