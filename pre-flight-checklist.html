Pre-Flight Checklist

Complete ALL items BEFORE workshop day. Tick each box when done.

  • Facebook personal account

    Log in at facebook.com and make sure it works

  • Facebook Business Page

    Don't have one? Create it at facebook.com/pages/create -- pick any name, you can change it later

  • Meta Ads Manager account

    Go to adsmanager.facebook.com -- you should see an empty dashboard. If it asks you to set up, follow the prompts.

  • Payment method linked in Ads Manager

    Ads Manager > gear icon > Billing & Payments > Add a card with at least $20. This is for your ad budget ($5-10/day). You can pause anytime.

  • Claude account

    Sign up at claude.ai (free tier is fine). Try asking it something to make sure it works.

  • Canva account

    Sign up at canva.com (free tier is fine)

  • Laptop packed (not tablet or phone)

    You'll need a full browser with multiple tabs open

  • Charger packed

    The workshop is 3+ hours -- bring your charger
