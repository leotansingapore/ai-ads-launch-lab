## Template 3: "How Much Insurance Do You Actually Need?"

**Cover Page:**
- Title: How Much Insurance Do You Actually Need?

- Footer: A Free Guide by [Your Name] | [Your Practice]

**Pages 2-6: Assessment Questions (one per page)**
1. What's your annual income? (Why it matters: your coverage should be 9-10x)
2. Do you have dependents? (Why it matters: life insurance needs change drastically)
3. What's your outstanding mortgage? (Why it matters: mortgage protection)
