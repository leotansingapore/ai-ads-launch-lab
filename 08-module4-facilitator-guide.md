# Module 4: Build & Launch Your Campaign -- Facilitator Guide (60 min)

Format: I do, you do. Every step demonstrated on projector first, then attendees follow.

Pace: Do each step, wait for the room to catch up, then move on. Say "Thumbs up when you're here" at each checkpoint.

---

## Step 1: Open Meta Ads Manager (2 min)

SAY: "Go to adsmanager.facebook.com in your browser. You should see your dashboard."

SHOW: Your Ads Manager dashboard on the projector.

CHECKPOINT: "Everyone should see an Ads Manager dashboard. If you see an error or a setup screen, raise your hand."

TROUBLESHOOT: If someone can't access it, have them try business.facebook.com first. If their account is restricted, they'll need to pair up with someone for now.

---

## Step 2: Create a New Campaign (3 min)

CLICK: Green **"+ Create"** button (top left)

SAY: "Click the green Create button."

SCREEN: Campaign objective selection

CLICK: Select **"Leads"**

SAY: "We're picking Leads because we want people to fill in a form -- not just visit a website."

CLICK: **"Continue"**

NAME: Type a campaign name. Tell them: "Name it something you'll recognize. For example: 'Retirement Guide Lead Gen' or 'CPF Checklist Leads'."

CHECKPOINT: "You should now see the Ad Set screen. Thumbs up."

---

## Step 3: Set Up the Ad Set -- Audience (10 min)

SAY: "This is where we tell Meta who should see your ad."

### Location
CLICK: Under "Locations", select **Singapore**
SAY: "Since we're all based in Singapore, keep this simple."

### Age
CLICK: Set age range
SAY: "Pick the age range of your ideal client. If you're not sure, go with 25-45. You can always change this later."

SUGGESTED: 25-45 for general financial planning

### Interests
CLICK: Under "Detailed Targeting", click **"Browse"**
SAY: "Now we add interests. This tells Meta to show your ad to people who are interested in these topics."

TYPE AND ADD THESE ONE BY ONE:
- Financial planning
- Investments
- Central Provident Fund (CPF)
- Retirement planning
- Insurance

SAY: "Pick 3-5 that match your lead magnet topic. If your guide is about retirement, add retirement-related interests. If it's about insurance, add insurance."

CHECKPOINT: "Your audience section should show Singapore, an age range, and 3-5 interests. Thumbs up."

---

## Step 4: Set Up the Ad Set -- Budget & Schedule (5 min)

### Budget
CLICK: Under "Budget & Schedule", select **"Daily Budget"**
TYPE: **$5** (or $10 if they're comfortable)

SAY: "$5 a day is enough to start. That's less than your kopi and lunch. You can always increase later."

### Schedule
CLICK: Set start date to **today**
CLICK: Set end date to **3 days from today**

SAY: "We'll run it for 3 days as a test. That gives Meta enough data to optimize. Don't judge results after day 1."

CHECKPOINT: "Budget should show $5/day, running for 3 days. Thumbs up."

---

## Step 5: Build the Ad -- Creative (15 min)

SAY: "Now we move to the Ad level. This is what people actually see in their feed."

### Choose Facebook Page
CLICK: Under "Identity", select your **Facebook Business Page**
SAY: "Pick the Facebook Page you created. This is who the ad comes from."

### Upload Image
SAY: "Remember the lead magnet cover you made in Canva? We're going to use that as your ad image."

CLICK: Under "Ad Creative", click **"Add Media" > "Add Image"**
CLICK: **Upload** the Canva cover image (the first page of their lead magnet, exported as PNG/JPG)

TIP: "If you didn't export just the cover page, go back to Canva, click on page 1, click Share > Download > PNG, download just that page, and upload it here."

CHECKPOINT: "You should see your lead magnet cover as the ad image. Thumbs up."

### Write Ad Copy with Claude
SAY: "Now open Claude in another tab. We're going to write your ad copy."

PROMPT (show on projector -- this is from the cheat sheet):
```
I'm running a Meta lead gen ad for my financial advisory practice in Singapore.

My lead magnet is: [THEIR LEAD MAGNET TITLE].
My target audience is 25-40 year old working adults in Singapore
interested in [THEIR TOPIC].

Write me:
1) Primary text (under 125 characters for above-the-fold)
2) Headline (under 40 characters)
3) Description (under 30 characters)

Keep it benefit-focused and conversational. Give me 3 variations.
```

SAY: "Replace the bracketed parts with your own details. Hit enter. Pick the variation you like best."

THEN PASTE INTO ADS MANAGER:
- **Primary text** -> "Primary text" field
- **Headline** -> "Headline" field
- **Description** -> "Description" field

CHECKPOINT: "You should see your image, your headline, and your text in the ad preview on the right. Thumbs up."

---

## Step 6: Create the Instant Form (10 min)

SAY: "Last piece -- the lead form. When someone clicks your ad, this form pops up inside Facebook."

CLICK: Under "Destination", select **"Instant Form"**
CLICK: **"Create Form"**

### Form Setup
- **Form Name:** "[Your Topic] Lead Form"
- **Form Type:** Select **"More Volume"** (not "Higher Intent" -- we want easy fills for beginners)

### Questions
CLICK: **"Add Question"** and set up these fields:
1. **Full Name** (pre-filled by Facebook)
2. **Phone Number** (pre-filled by Facebook)
3. **Custom question:** "What's your biggest financial concern right now?"
   - Type: Short answer

SAY: "Facebook auto-fills their name and phone from their profile. They just have to type one answer. Less friction = more leads."

### Thank You Screen
- Headline: "Thanks! I'll be in touch soon."
- Description: "I'll reach out within 24 hours to schedule a quick chat."
- Button: "Visit Website" (optional -- can link to your Facebook page or leave default)

CLICK: **"Publish"** (saves the form)

CHECKPOINT: "Your form should be attached to the ad. You should see it in the ad preview. Thumbs up."

---

## Step 7: Review Everything Together (5 min)

SAY: "Before we launch, let's review. Your campaign should have:"

Run through this checklist on the projector:
- [ ] Campaign objective: Leads
- [ ] Location: Singapore
- [ ] Age range set
- [ ] 3-5 interests added
- [ ] Budget: $5-10/day
- [ ] Schedule: 3 days starting today
- [ ] Ad image: your lead magnet cover
- [ ] Ad copy: from Claude (primary text, headline, description)
- [ ] Instant Form: name, phone, custom question

SAY: "Check each one. If something's missing, fix it now. Raise your hand if you need help."

Give 2-3 minutes for final fixes.

---

## Step 8: Launch! (5 min)

SAY: "Is everyone ready?"

COUNTDOWN: "Your first ever Meta ad campaign. 3... 2... 1... click Publish."

CLICK: **"Publish"** button

SAY: "It's live. Meta will review it -- usually takes 15-60 minutes. You might see 'In Review' status. That's normal. Once approved, it starts running."

CELEBRATE: "You just did something 95% of financial advisors have never done. You built a lead generation system using AI. Give yourselves a round of applause."

---

## Common Issues During This Module

| Issue | Fix |
|-------|-----|
| "I can't find the Create button" | Top left corner of Ads Manager. Green button. |
| "My Page isn't showing up" | They may not be admin of their Page. Check Page settings. |
| "The image is wrong size" | Canva exports at correct size by default. If cropped weird, re-export as 1080x1080. |
| "Claude gave me copy that's too long" | Ask Claude: "Make the primary text shorter, under 125 characters" |
| "My ad was rejected" | Common for finance. Usually policy review. Click "Edit" and check for restricted words (guaranteed returns, etc). Remove and resubmit. |
| "Form won't save" | Make sure all required fields are filled. Try a different browser if stuck. |
| Someone is way behind | Pair them with a neighbor who's done. Or have them watch your screen and catch up after. |
