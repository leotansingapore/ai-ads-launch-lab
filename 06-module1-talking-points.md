# Module 1: Why AI + Ads (20 min)

Format: You talking, no laptops. Projector on for demo.

---

## OPENING (3 min)

"How many of you have ever run a Facebook or Instagram ad?"
(Expect few or no hands)

"How many of you have used ChatGPT or Claude before?"
(Probably more hands)

"By the end of today, you'll combine both -- you'll have a real ad campaign running, generating leads for your practice, built with AI. And it'll take you less time than your last prospecting session."

---

## YOUR STORY (5 min)

Tell your own journey. Hit these beats:

- "When I started, I was doing what everyone does -- cold calling, door knocking, asking friends and family for referrals."
- Share your specific numbers: [X] calls per day, [Y]% pick-up rate, [Z] appointments per week
- "Then I tried running ads. First campaign: $5 a day. I got [X] leads in the first week."
- "Today, my team generates leads while we sleep. Here's what that looks like."

**[Show screenshot on projector]**
- Your real Meta Ads Manager dashboard
- Point to: campaign name, cost per lead, total leads, date range
- "This is not a course screenshot. This is my actual account."

Key line: "I'm not showing you this to impress you. I'm showing you because in 3 hours, you're going to build your own version of this."

---

## THE MATH (5 min)

**Cold calling:**
- 100 calls -> ~30 pick up -> ~5 willing to meet -> ~1-2 become clients
- Time: 2-3 hours of calling
- Emotional cost: high rejection

**$10/day Meta ad:**
- Runs 24/7, even while you're with clients
- 1-3 leads per day delivered to your phone
- They opted in -- they WANT to hear from you
- Time: 30 minutes to set up, then it runs itself

"Which one would you rather do? That's what today is about."

---

## LIVE DEMO (5 min)

Open Claude (claude.ai) on the projector.

Type this prompt live -- let them watch the AI work:

```
I'm a financial advisor in Singapore. Help me write a short lead magnet PDF
(5-7 key points) on the topic: "5 Retirement Mistakes Singaporeans Make
Before 40". My target audience is working adults aged 25-40. Keep the tone
practical, friendly, and jargon-free.
```

As Claude generates the response, narrate:
- "Look at that -- 30 seconds and you have a complete lead magnet draft."
- "Writing that yourself? 30 minutes minimum. And it probably wouldn't be as structured."
- "This is the AI advantage. And you're about to use it."

---

## SET EXPECTATIONS (2 min)

"In the next 3 hours, you're going to:

1. Build a lead magnet using AI -- a PDF guide you can offer to prospects
2. Create a Meta ad campaign that promotes it
3. Launch it. Today. Live. From this room.

You don't need to be tech-savvy. You don't need marketing experience. I'm going to do every step on this screen first, and then you follow along on your laptop.

Open your laptops. Let's go."

---

3. The math: cold calling vs. ads (side by side)
4. "Open your laptops" (transition slide)

Keep slides minimal. This module runs on your energy and credibility, not slides.
