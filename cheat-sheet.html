Your AI Prompt Cheat Sheet (ChatGPT or Claude)

1
Generate Your Lead Magnet Content
I'm a financial advisor in Singapore. Help me write a short lead magnet PDF (5-7 key points) on the topic: [TOPIC]. My target audience is working adults aged 25-40 in Singapore. Keep the tone practical, friendly, and jargon-free. Include a brief intro, the main points, and a closing that invites them to book a free consultation.
Replace [TOPIC] with your chosen topic, e.g. "5 Retirement Mistakes Singaporeans Make Before 40"
1b
Design Your Guide + Get the PDF (same chat)
Now turn this guide into a beautifully designed document. Build it as one self-contained HTML file I can download. Design: navy blue and gold, modern, generous spacing, large readable text. A full-page cover with the title and "A free guide by [YOUR NAME]", one section per point, and a closing page with a "Book your free consultation" call to action plus my contact: [PHONE] / [EMAIL]. Make it print-perfect: A4 pages, each section starting on a new page, print styles included. Give me the finished .html file to download.
Both tools: download the file, double-click to open it in your browser, Cmd+P → Save as PDF. Don't ask for a PDF file directly; the HTML route is what looks designed.
1c
Cover Image (ChatGPT)
Create a square 1:1 cover image for a free PDF guide called "[GUIDE TITLE]". Professional financial-services style, navy blue and gold, the title in large bold text, a small "FREE GUIDE" badge, clean simple background, no people, space around the text.
Check the spelling in the image; if the title is garbled, say "regenerate with the exact text [TITLE]". This image becomes your ad image in Meta.
2
Write Your Ad Copy
I'm running a Meta lead gen ad for my financial advisory practice in Singapore. My lead magnet is: [LEAD MAGNET TITLE]. My target audience is [AGE RANGE] year old working adults in Singapore interested in [TOPIC]. Write me: 1) Primary text (under 125 characters for above-the-fold) 2) Headline (under 40 characters) 3) Description (under 30 characters) Keep it benefit-focused and conversational. Give me 3 variations.
3
Write Your Lead Follow-Up Message
Someone just filled in my Meta lead form. Their details: - Name: [NAME] - Phone: [PHONE] - Biggest financial concern: [CONCERN] Write me a short WhatsApp message (under 100 words) to follow up. Be warm, reference their specific concern, and suggest a quick 15-min call. No hard sell.

Pro Tips

  • Replace [BRACKETED TEXT] with your own details before sending
  • Add "Give me 3 versions" to any prompt for options
  • Say "Make it more casual" or "Make it shorter" to adjust
  • If the first result isn't right, tell Claude what to change -- just iterate
  • Save your best prompts -- you'll reuse them every new campaign
