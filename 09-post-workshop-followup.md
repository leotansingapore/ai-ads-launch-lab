## Message 2: Day 3 Check-In (T+3)

---

Hi [Name],

Your campaign has been running for a few days now. How's it going?

Quick check:
- Have you received any leads? (Check Ads Manager > your campaign > Results)
- If yes -- have you followed up with them?
- If no leads yet -- that's okay. Reply with a screenshot of your campaign and I'll take a look.

Common questions at this stage:
- "My cost per lead is high" -- Normal for the first few days. Meta is still learning. Give it 5-7 days total.
- "I got leads but they don't reply" -- Follow up faster (within 1-2 hours) and try calling instead of just messaging.
- "My ad was rejected" -- Remove any words like "guaranteed", "100%", or income claims. Resubmit.

Keep it running. The data gets better every day.

Leo
