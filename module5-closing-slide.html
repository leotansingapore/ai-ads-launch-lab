The rules

The 48-Hour Rule

Don't touch your ad for 48 hours. Meta's algorithm needs time to learn. Changing things too early resets the learning.

When to Kill an Ad

After 48 hours + 500 impressions with zero leads. Edit the copy or image and relaunch. Don't delete -- edit.

When to Scale

If your cost per lead is under $10 for 3+ days, increase budget by 20%. Don't double it overnight.

Ad Rejected?

Normal for finance. Remove words like "guaranteed", "100%", or income claims. Edit and resubmit. Not a failure.
