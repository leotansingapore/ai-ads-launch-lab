## Message 2: Troubleshooting Reply (Use when attendees reply with setup issues)

### Issue: Can't access Ads Manager

No worries -- this is the most common issue. Here's the quick fix:

1. Go to business.facebook.com
2. Click "Create Account" if you don't have one
3. Then go to adsmanager.facebook.com
4. Go to Billing > Payment Methods > Add your card

If your account says "restricted" or "disabled", it may need identity verification. Go to Settings > Identity Confirmation and follow the steps. This can take 24-48 hours, so do it now.

Still stuck? Send me a screenshot of what you see and I'll walk you through it.

### Issue: Don't have a Facebook Business Page

Easy fix:

1. Go to facebook.com/pages/create
2. Pick a name (e.g., "[Your Name] -- Financial Advisory")
3. Category: "Financial Planner" or "Financial Service"
4. Add a profile photo (a headshot is fine)
5. Done -- you don't need followers or posts for this workshop

### Issue: Don't have a laptop

Unfortunately you'll need a laptop for this workshop -- phones and tablets won't work well with Meta Ads Manager. If you can borrow one from a friend or colleague, that would work. Let me know if this is a blocker.
