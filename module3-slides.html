Lead Form Ads win

Traffic ad: See ad -> Click -> Go to website -> Find form -> Fill it in -> ...maybe

Lead form ad: See ad -> Click -> Form pops up inside Facebook -> Done

Why this matters:

  • > No website needed
  • > Facebook auto-fills their name and email
  • > Fewer steps = more people complete it

Targeting for Financial Advisors

  • Location: Singapore
  • Age: 25-45 (adjust to your niche)
  • Interests: Financial planning, Investments, CPF, Retirement, Insurance

Pro tip

Start broad. Don't over-target. Meta's algorithm is smart -- it'll find the right people. The more you restrict, the more expensive each lead becomes.
