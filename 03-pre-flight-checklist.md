# AI Ads Launch Lab -- Pre-Flight Checklist

Complete ALL items BEFORE workshop day. If you get stuck, reply to this message and we'll help you sort it out.

---

[ ] I have a Facebook personal account and can log in
    - facebook.com -- try logging in now

[ ] I have a Facebook Business Page
    - Don't have one? Go to facebook.com/pages/create
    - Pick any name and category for now -- you can change it later

[ ] I have a Meta Ads Manager account
    - Go to adsmanager.facebook.com
    - If it asks you to create one, follow the prompts
    - You should see an empty dashboard -- that means it's working

[ ] My Ads Manager has a payment method linked
    - In Ads Manager: click the gear icon > Billing & Payments > Payment Methods
    - Add a credit or debit card with at least $20 available
    - This is for your ad budget ($5-10/day) -- you can pause anytime

[ ] I have a Claude account and can log in
    - Go to claude.ai and sign up (free tier is fine)
    - Try asking it something to make sure it works

[ ] I have a Canva account and can log in
    - Go to canva.com and sign up (free tier is fine)

[ ] I'm bringing my laptop (not tablet, not phone)

[ ] My laptop charger is packed
